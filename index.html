Features

Chat System

The CampusConnect chat system enables real-time, one-on-one conversations between students and alumni. Built using Supabase’s real-time database and authentication features, it allows users to send and receive messages instantly in a secure and private environment. Whether it’s for career guidance, mentorship, or friendly catch-ups, the chat interface is designed to be fast, responsive, and easy to use—bringing conversations to life within your campus network.

Community

The community feed allows users to share updates, post announcements, celebrate achievements, and engage in open discussion. It functions as a social timeline where both students and alumni can contribute to the ongoing story of their campus. Powered by Supabase’s real-time data handling, the feed ensures that new posts appear instantly, keeping the community connected, active, and informed.

Dashboard

The CampusConnect dashboard is the central hub for every user. Once logged in, users are greeted with a personalized view that displays key information such as their profile, recent activity, and quick links to explore the community feed, alumni directory, and active chats. Designed for clarity and ease of access, the dashboard acts as the starting point for users to navigate their digital campus experience efficiently.

Global Search

The alumni directory is a powerful tool for discovering and connecting with verified alumni from various departments, batches, and locations. Users can filter alumni by profession, college, department, location, or batch and even search using keywords. Each alumni profile includes key information and a “Connect” button to initiate chat. This directory is the backbone of CampusConnect's networking feature—bridging the gap between alumni and students.
